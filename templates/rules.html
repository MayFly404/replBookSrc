Rules:

  1. On referring to other users in anything but nice terms.
  2. No content that is strictly school appropriate, that includes any word associated with violence, NSFW content, or anything that is in contradiction with school rules and policies.
  3. No sharing personal information
  4. No cursing
  5. YCDSocial Admins have the ability to directly edit and delete posts, if any of the above rules are broken we will delete the post.

How to use:

  1. To make a new blog you hit “create a new blog”
  2. To make a profile you put in your email, we recommend using your YCDS email and put in a password. This is not recorded.
  3. To respond to a blog you can say “re:___” or, if they include the blog editing password in the post, you can directly edit their post.
  4. The live chat function is a function where you can create a room, and with your friends, can chat. To make a room you make a room name and then share it with your friends to join. When you delete the page, however, the messages previously sent will not be saved.
